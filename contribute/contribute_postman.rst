How to contribute a Postman collection
======================================

Naming Conventions
---------------------
#.  Use the follow naming conventions [fqdn]-[optional tags]-create and [fqdn]-[optional tags]-delete. For example:

  - vpn.acme.com-ad-create 
  - vpn.acme.com-ad-delete
